Question: The patient presents for cleft lip repair, and underwent Millard technique, which consisted of rotating the medial segment and advancing the lateral flap. The Cupids bow with piltrum is preserved with this technique. What is the appropriate root operation for a cleft lip repair using the Millard Rotation Advancement technique? 
Answer: Assign code 0KX10Z2, Transfer facial muscle with skin and subcutaneous tissue, open approach, for the rotation advancement procedure. A transfer is “moving, without taking out, all or a portion of a body part to another location to take over the function of all or a portion of a body part.” In this case, a muscle flap was anastomosed across the cleft which covered the defect. The body part transferred remained connected to its vascular supply.
