Question: The patient developed a nonhealing necrotic ulcer of the right thigh. Using a VersaJet debrider, the physician performed debridement of the skin and subcutaneous tissue. How should this procedure be coded? 
Answer: Assign code 0HDHXZZ, Extraction of right upper leg skin, external approach, for the nonexcisional debridement using VersaJet. The VersaJet is a device consisting of an ultra high-pressure generator with a console and disposable attachments. A natural vacuum created by the jet stream removes tissue fragments. The device has specialized features that allow physicians to debride traumatic wounds, chronic wounds, or other soft tissue lesions and aspirate and remove contaminants or other debris. Debridement with Versajet debrider is coded as nonexcisional debridement.
