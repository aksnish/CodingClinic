Question: A patient was taken to the operating room for laparoscopic revision of her gastric band. Following the revision, the area of the tubing as well as the left upper quadrant was instilled with a solution made of saline and Seprafilm to minimize the chance of adhesions in the future. What is the correct approach value for the introduction of the saline and Seprafilm solution? 
Answer: In this case, the wound was treated with a solution consisting of saline and Seprafilm, which was spread throughout the peritoneal cavity. An adhesion barrier was not actually placed in the peritoneal cavity. Although a code is not required, if desired, assign the following ICD-10-PCS code: 3E0M3GC
