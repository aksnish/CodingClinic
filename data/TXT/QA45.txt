Question: A patient had laparoscopic partial left salpingectomy to treat left tubal pregnancy. During surgery, a portion of the left fallopian tube, its ectopic pregnancy, and the mesosalpinx were coagulated and cut. The ectopic pregnancy and fallopian tube were removed. What are the correct body system and root operation for the left laparoscopic partial salpingectomy for left tubal ectopic pregnancy? 
Answer: For the laparoscopic removal of the ectopic products of conception, and laparoscopic left partial salpingectomy, assign the following ICD-10-PCS codes respectively: 10T24ZZ
