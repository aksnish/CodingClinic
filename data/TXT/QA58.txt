Question: The patient had multiple drug-eluting stents placed percutaneously in three distinct lesions occurring in three separate coronary arteries: the first diagonal (D1) branch of the left anterior descending (LAD), the left circumflex (LCX), and the right coronary artery (RCA). What is the appropriate body part value for coronary artery sites(s)? Is this considered three coronary artery sites? 
Answer: Yes, three coronary artery sites were treated. Assign code 027234Z, Dilation of coronary artery, three sites, with drug-eluting intraluminal device, percutaneous approach, for the treatment of three coronary artery sites, the LAD, the LCX, and the RCA with insertion of drug-eluting stents.
