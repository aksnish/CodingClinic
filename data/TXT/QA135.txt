Question: A five-year-old presents with a rib deformity associated with congenital scoliosis affecting the thoracic region with thoracic insufficiency. The patient underwent open placement of vertical expandable prosthetic titanium rib (VEPTR), involving two left ribs. What are the appropriate code assignments? 
Answer: Assign code M95.4, Acquired deformity of chest and ribs, for the rib deformity as the principal diagnosis. Assign code Q67.5, Congenital deformity of spine, for the congenital scoliosis, as an additional diagnosis. Assign the following ICD-10-PCS code for the VEPTR placement to reposition the two left ribs: 0PS204Z
