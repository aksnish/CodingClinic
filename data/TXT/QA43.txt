Question: A 22-year-old female presents for cerclage removal. She has a history of cerclage placement due to cervical incompetence, and is status post cesarean delivery seven months ago. Cerclage removal was attempted at the time of labor and delivery, but was unsuccessful. At surgery, she underwent hysteroscopic removal of the cerclage. What is the appropriate ICD-10-PCS code for removal of the cerclage? 
Answer: A cerclage is a suture, not a device. The appropriate root operation is “Extirpation.” Assign the following ICD-10-PCS code for the removal of cervical cerclage via hysteroscopy: 0UCC8ZZ
